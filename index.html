Dear Subscriber,

 

We’re delighted to invite you to the next installment of The New Yorker Live, our interactive video event series exclusively for subscribers.

 

To mark the magazine’s ninety-seventh anniversary, the cover artists Kadir Nelson and Malika Favre will join its art editor, Françoise Mouly, to discuss the creation of some of The New Yorker’s most memorable covers, including their own, and how the covers reflect changing racial, gender, and cultural mores. “The Art of the New Yorker Cover” will take place on Thursday, February 10th at 6 P.M. E.T.

 

We encourage you to register so that you will receive an e-mail notification when the event is about to begin. You must be signed in to newyorker.com/live to view the live stream on the event page and to participate in the question-and-answer session. We look forward to (virtually) welcoming you!
